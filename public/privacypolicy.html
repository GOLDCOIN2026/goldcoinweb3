Privacy Policy for GOLDCOIN GAME

Last updated: October 2025

Welcome to GOLDCOIN GAME. This privacy policy explains how we handle user data within the app. Our goal is to provide a fun and fair gaming experience while respecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: We use Firebase Authentication to let you sign up or log in. Your email or login method is stored securely to identify your account and progress.
  • Gameplay Data: We store your in-game progress (coins, leaderboard score, referrals) in our database to maintain your game history.
  • Device Information: AdMob may collect basic device and usage information for ad personalization and analytics.

2. How We Use Information

  • To maintain your game progress and account.
  • To show relevant ads through AdMob.
  • To improve game balance and user experience.

3. Ads and Third Parties

We use Google AdMob to serve ads within the app. AdMob may use cookies or identifiers to personalize ads. For more details, visit Google Privacy Policy.

4. Game Currency (Coins)

All coins in GOLDCOIN GAME are virtual only and have no real-world value. Coins can be used to play games, spins, and other in-app features. They cannot be exchanged for money or prizes.

5. Security

We take your privacy seriously and store all data securely in Firebase and Firestore using encrypted connections.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages, but users under 13 should have parental supervision when using online features.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page.
