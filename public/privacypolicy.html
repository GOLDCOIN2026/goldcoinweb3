Privacy Policy for GOLDCOIN GAME

Last updated: October 2025

Welcome to GOLDCOIN GAME. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information while providing a fun and fair gaming experience.

1. Information We Collect

  • Game Play: User Can Play Free Game without Login. The Progress will be saved in local storage. Scores details will be available till keeping the same device.
  • Account Information: We use Firebase Authentication to let you sign up or log in. Your email or login method is stored securely to identify your account and save your progress.
  • Gameplay Data: Your in-game progress (coins, leaderboard rank, invites, achievements) is stored securely in Firestore.
  • Device Information: AdMob may collect limited device and usage information for ad personalization and analytics.

2. How We Use Information

  • To Authentice Users, we use Google firebase (Trusted/Encrypted Auth System).
  • To maintain your account and game progress.
  • To show relevant ads via Google AdMob.
  • To improve app stability, fairness, and gameplay balance.

3. Ads and Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may use cookies or advertising identifiers to deliver personalized ads. For details, visit Google’s Privacy Policy.

4. Game Coins and Leaderboard

All coins in GOLDCOIN GAME are virtual and for entertainment only. They cannot be exchanged for money or prizes. Coins are not used anywhere except showing only score and projection in leaderboard. In future updates, it may be redeemed for inapp skins, avaters and unlocking features. Your leaderboard rank only reflects your in-game performance.

5. Your Privacy Controls

  • Password Reset: Account password reset is available anytime on the Profile Page, allowing you to securely change your password yourself.
  • Account Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from the Profile Page at any time.
  • Delete via Email: If you are unable to log in and wish to delete your account, simply email us at goldcoinweb3@gmail.com. Your account and all related data will be permanently deleted within 7 days of your request.

6. Security

All user data is stored securely in Firebase and Firestore using encrypted connections. We take reasonable measures to protect against unauthorized access or misuse.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. However, users under 13 should use the app under parental supervision when accessing online features.
